Naruto Uzumaki

Naruto Uzumaki is a young ninja with dreams of becoming Hokage. He is the main character of the anime Naruto.

Ichigo Kurosaki

Ichigo Kurosaki is a teenager with the ability to see ghosts. He is the main character of the anime Bleach.

Asta

Asta is the main character of the anime Black Clover. He is a non-magic user striving to become the Wizard King.

Isagi Yoichi

Isagi Yoichi is the main character of the manga Bluelock. He strives for victory in the world of football as an egoist.

Itadori Yuiji

Itadori Yuiji is the main character of the anime Jujutsu Kaisen. He becomes involved in a world of curses and sorcery.

Eren Yeager

Eren Yeager is the main character of the anime Attack on Titan. He seeks freedom and revenge against the Titans.
